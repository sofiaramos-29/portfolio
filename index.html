§ 05 About

Background.

Developer by logic.
Designer by instinct.

I started writing HTML at 13, not for school, just out of curiosity. That curiosity never stopped. Now 20, I am a 1st year BSIT student at Informatics College Northgate while running an active freelance practice across web development, graphic design, video editing, and academic assistance.

In senior high school, I rose to Associate Editor in Chief of The Cerulean, our school publication, overseeing visual identity, editorial layout, and design direction throughout Grade 12, after serving as Head Graphic Designer in Grade 11. Graduated with a GWA of 94.5.

Client work spans local businesses and individual clients who need digital products that look intentional and hold up long after the handoff. My approach is straightforward: understand the problem first, build something clean, and make sure it works without me in the room.

Outside project work, I also assist students as an academic guidance provider, breaking down complex course material into concepts that actually stick. Think less tutor, more translator.
